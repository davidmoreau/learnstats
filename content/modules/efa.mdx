---
title: "Exploratory Factor Analysis (EFA)"
description: "Use correlation patterns and eigenvalues to infer latent dimensionality."
---

EFA looks for a small set of latent factors that explain correlation among observed variables.

<EFADemo />

## Key idea

The scree plot and loading pattern help decide whether one broad factor or multiple factors better represent the data.
