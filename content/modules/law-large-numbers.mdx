---
title: "Law of Large Numbers"
description: "Running averages settle down toward the true expected value as trials increase."
---

The Law of Large Numbers says that, with repeated independent draws, the sample average converges to the true expectation.

<LawOfLargeNumbersDemo />

## Reading the Demo

- The blue curve is the running mean.
- The red dashed line is the true probability `p`.
- Early variability is expected; stability improves as trial count grows.
