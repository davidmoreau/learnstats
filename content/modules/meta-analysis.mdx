Meta-analysis combines evidence across studies. Precision-weighted pooling gives more influence to studies with smaller standard errors.

<MetaAnalysisDemo />

## Practical Takeaway

A pooled estimate can be more stable than single studies, but heterogeneity matters. High `I²` suggests real between-study differences.
