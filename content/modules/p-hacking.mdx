P-hacking happens when researchers try many analyses and report whichever crosses `p < alpha`. Even with no true effect, selective reporting inflates false positives.

<PHackingDemo />

## Practical Takeaway

Pre-registering the primary outcome and analysis plan helps keep the false positive rate near its nominal target.
